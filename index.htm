cMaps

1. To export your cMap, go to (File/Export CMap As/HTML Page)
2. Save it to a new folder in your H:/cMaps/cmap name


GitHub

1. Go to Github.com
2. Create a new account with you school email and a password of your choosing.
       -For username use lastname then first initial(ex. funkb for Brandon Funk)
3. Go to mail.google.com and confirm your github account
4. Create a new repository named "[yourusername].github.io"
5. Don't fill anything else out and click create repository.
6. In your new repository, near the top right, click "Upload Files"
7. Drag your new folder into "Drag files here" area
